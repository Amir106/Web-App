League of Legends

The first game I really enjoy playing is League of Legends. It is a really fun game to play online with your friends or alone. The game is played 5v5 on a map called Summoners Rift. The point of the game is to eliminate the opposite teams turrets and eventually destroy their nexus (the main point). There are over 140 characters which from you can choose, and there are 5 roles from which every one plays an important part. This game is based on teamwork and individual skill to outclass your opponent making you win the game.


Counter Strike: Global Offensive

The second game that I really enjoy playing is Counter Strike: Global Offensive. It is an FPS game which is also played 5v5 on many maps. There are 2 teams. The first team is Counter-Terrorist and the other team is the Terrorist team. The terrorist team needs to plant the bomb on one of the 2 sites which are on every map and survive to the detonation in order to win the round while the CT team needs to either prevent the Terrorists from planting or in the case they plant the bomb, they need to defuse it. The game is played in 30 rounds and each team is on one side for 15 rounds then they swap. The winner is the first team to gather 16 won rounds, in case of a 15-15 the game is over as a tie. The game is really enjoyable and I have made quite a few friends over it.

Call of Duty Series

Call of Duty is a first-person shooter video game franchise published by Activision. The game has many editions each put in a different time period. From WW1 and WW2 to modern even future warfare this game has it all. The game is famous for its campaign mode which has unique war stories with various characters and we get to experience war from their views.


The game has amazing graphics and physics. It can be played offline and online and is one of the most sold game series in the world.

Fifa Series

FIFA is probably the worlds most famous football simulation game. There is not much more to say. Every year there is a new instalation with updated teams.
