Biography

I was born on 10th of June in 1998 in Sarajevo, the capital of Bosnia and Herzegovina. I grew up in a good neighbourhood with a loving and caring family. Ever since I was a little boy I have been interested in computers and technology as a whole. From the year my uncle bought me my first computer I knew that this profession is what I wanted to do.

My primary school is called "Musa Cazim Catic", and it is said to be one of the hardest schools in the city. After finishing primary school in 2013, I headed on into middle school. I finished Druga gimnazija Sarajevo in 2017 and continued into college entering the International University of Sarajevo.

This is the present chapter of my life. I started attending IUS in 2017 and am currently on the third year of Computer Science studies. I have finished the first and the second year with no problems and hope to continue this trend (if you pass me) this year.

The first project that I've done is a video game web-site where I post my reviews of the games I've played.

The second project that I've done is a web-site for my friends music band named Kapodaster.
